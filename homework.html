FOOTBALL

Football is the world's most popular sport, featuring two teams of 11 players trying to score by moving a ball into the opponent's goal, primarily using their feet.

It combines physical exercise, mental alertness, and social teamwork, keeping players fit while providing a fun escape.

The most common and widely recognized football in a conference refers to structured groupings of teams within a larger league, primarily seen in the National Football League (NFL).
